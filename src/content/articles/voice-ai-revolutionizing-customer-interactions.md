## How It Works in Real Time

1. A caller dials in, and the call is routed through Telephony.

2. The LLM processes the speech, understands the intent, and generates a response.

3. The Voice API converts the AI's response into natural-sounding speech.

4. If enabled, the Transcription API records and transcribes the conversation for QA and monitoring.

5. The caller receives a real-time response no delays, no robotic interactions.
